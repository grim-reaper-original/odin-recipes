Poha


Description

Idlies are one of the most popular culinary choices in South India. The ease of preperation, the versatility of pairing it with various side dishes, as well as the just the savoury taste in general, makes it a truly wonderful creation of mankind

Now, how do we go about making this absolutely mouthwatering dish? So glad you're here to learn.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups Poha rice
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Peeled Potato
  • 1/3 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves
  • 1 Finely Chopped Green Chilli
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 1/2 tsp Chopped Peanuts

    • Steps

    1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 1.5 cup poha. do not take thin poha as it turns mushy as soon as you add water.
    2. Rinse in water and drain off the water.
    3. Add 1 tsp sugar, ¾ tsp salt and ¼ tsp turmeric.
    4. Mix gently using your fingers without making poha mushy.
    5. Rest for 8-10 minutes, or until the poha softens without turning mushy or sticky.
    6. In a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and roast 2 tbsp peanut on low flame.
    7. Roast until the peanuts turn crunchy, keep aside.
    8. In the same oil, add 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp cumin, pinch hing and few curry leaves. splutter the tempering.
    9. Now add 2 chilli, 1 inch ginger and 1 onion. saute until the onions softens slightly.
    10. Add ¼ tsp turmeric and ¼ tsp salt. saute slightly.
    11. Add soaked poha and mix well combining everything well.
    12. Cover and simmer for 3 minutes or unitl the poha is cooked well.
    13. Now add fried peanuts, 2 tbsp coriander and ½ lemon. mix well.
    14. Finally, enjoy kanda poha recipe with the mixture as a morning breakfast.
